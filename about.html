About Us!

The designers behind Quiet Scape are two aspiring young professionals, Samson Yang  and Zïa Bailey looking to solve our everyday decision making difficulties through a seamless user experience. As designers that could be considered “Boston Locals”, we have personally selected these locations we call “scapes” that we think best represent our general needs (this will be a growing list). We have been mentored by the head Web and Design Boston University Professor, Jon Surmacz and are continuing to improve our design skills to improve our website for you 😊.
